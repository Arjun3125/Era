CHRIS VOSS is one of the preeminent practitioners and professors of negotiating skills in the world. He currently teaches at both the University of Southern California's Marshall School of Business and Georgetown University's McDonough School of Business. Chris has lectured at many other preeminent universities, including Harvard Law School, the Sloan School of Management, and the Kellogg School of Management. He lives in Los Angeles, California.
TAHL RAZ uncovers big ideas and great stories that ignite change and growth in people and organizations.
