The human being is an imitator. They are constantly repeating the patterns of their parents, even if they do not realize it. The first step to break free from this cycle is to get rid of your parents, not physically, but by getting rid of the parental voices inside you. You must efface them and become free for the first time.

Every person feels resentful towards their parents because of the harm they have done. However, it is essential to understand that they wished all good for you and wanted everything for your well-being. But what can they do? Just by wanting something or having good wishes, nothing happens. They were well-wishers, but they are robots who create an atmosphere in which their children will eventually become like them.

To break the cycle of imitation, you must get rid of your parents. This requires careful observation and behavioral changes. Watch when your mother is there functioning through you and stop that. Do something absolutely new that your mother could not even have imagined.
